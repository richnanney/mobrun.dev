Glaceon is an Ice/Poison-type Pokémon introduced in Generation IV. It evolves from Eevee when exposed to an Ice Stone.

Biology

Glaceon is a mammalian, quadrupedal Pokémon covered in purple fur. This Stunderian variant has adapted to harsh, frozen environments and developed toxic abilities alongside its ice powers.
